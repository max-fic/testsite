Ableitung

$$
f'(x) = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{f(x+h) - f(x)}{h}
$$

$$
\begin{eqnarray}
(x^2)'& = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{(x+h)^2-x^2}{h}\\
& = \frac{x^2 + 2xh + h^2 - x^2}{h}\\
& = \frac{2xh+h^2}{h} = 2x + h = 2x\\
\end{eqnarray}
$$
